Undone

They ignored the new boy,
snickering behind his back.

In silence, I stayed safe

MyLipsPressedTogether.

Growing bolder,
they pierced him
with arrow-sharp
words.

I pretended I hadn’t heard.

They twisted his arms.
One word escaped his lips before they dragged him
out of sight,
out of earshot:
“Cowards!”

I ran away.

All the words. I didn't say

haunt me every day.
